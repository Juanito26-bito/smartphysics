Mechanical beats — direct visualization

Characterizing linear light sources

Acoustic characterization of magnetic braking
Acoustic methods via smartphone microphone used to characterise the velocity-dependent braking force in a magnetic damper. The Physics Teacher 60, 706–707.

#62Microphone · Sonar
Acoustic determination of g
Smartphone used as a sonar — emitting a sound pulse and tracking its echo off a free-falling body — to determine gravitational acceleration. Phys. Educ. 58, 035011.

#63Barometer
Barometric study of a draining container
Pressure sensor characterises how air pressure inside a sealed draining container evolves over time, connecting fluid dynamics with gas laws. Phys. Educ. 58, 055017.
2024
🎙️
#69Microphone
Inverse-square law via IR remote control
Acoustic detection of an IR remote's signal strength at varying distances tests the inverse-square law in a novel optics-acoustics hybrid experiment. Physics Education 59, 035016.

#70Signal Generator
Q-factor of a series RLC circuit
Smartphone acts as a function generator to sweep frequency across an RLC circuit, allowing precise measurement of the quality factor. Physics Education 59, 065023.

#71Sig. Gen + Oscilloscope

Wireless Li-Fi signal generator for RC circuits
A Li-Fi (light-based wireless) signal generator built with a smartphone sends driving signals to a series RC circuit without any physical connections. The Physics Teacher 62, 426–427.
